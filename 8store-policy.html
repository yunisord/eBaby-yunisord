Store Policy

Welcome to our store policy page. Our goal is to provide you with the best possible customer service. If you have any questions or concerns, please feel free to contact us.

Returns - We accept returns within 30 days of purchase. All returns must be in new and unused condition, with all original tags and packaging attached. Shipping charges are non-refundable. Refunds will be processed within 3-5 business days of receiving your return. You will be notified via email once your refund has been processed.

Exchanges - We accept exchanges within 30 days of purchase. All exchanges must be in new and unused condition, with all original tags and packaging attached. Shipping charges are non-refundable. To exchange an item, please contact us at [email protected] to request a return authorization. Once your return is authorized, you will be provided with return shipping instructions.

Damages - We take great care in packaging and shipping your order, but sometimes damages happen. If you receive a damaged item, please contact us within 48 hours of delivery at [email protected] We will work with you to resolve the issue as quickly as possible.
